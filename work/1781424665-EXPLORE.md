### 2.2 Candid assessment of the two remaining honest directions (T13)

#### (b) Attack a concrete SO-L² break condition

SO-L² records the wall in analytic-uniformity terms. There are exactly two pre-recorded
break/escalate conditions, each a genuine PROVIDES if achieved:

- **(b1) A shift-surviving variance / second-moment / large-sieve bound for the
  non-multiplicative `t`.** *Assessment: very hard, and the obstruction is structural,
  not merely unattempted.* The reason `L²` is normally easier is multiplicativity (the
  large sieve / Barban–Davenport–Halberstam variance bounds are powered by it). For
  `t(n)=λ(n)λ(n+2)` the off-diagonal of the variance `Σ_a S_{a,d}²` is itself a sum of
  **four-fold Liouville correlations at shifts `{0,2,kd,kd+2}`** (audited as V3b in
  `work/1781396231-EXPLORE.md`) — i.e. the second moment **regenerates higher
  correlations of the same family** rather than collapsing to a free diagonal plus error.
  So a "shift-surviving variance bound" is not a small perturbation of existing technology;
  it would itself be a new unconditional input about multi-point Liouville correlations
  uniform in `d`, which is in the same hardness class as C5. **Not tractable as a
  near-term `PROVIDES` by importing an existing tool;** it is essentially a restatement of
  the difficulty. (Honest: I see no concrete partial lemma here that is both new and
  audit-able short of solving the family.)

- **(b2) Remove the scale-averaging in Tao 2015.** *Assessment: this is the single most
  precisely localized open sub-problem in the whole graph, but it is a recognized hard
  open problem, not a near-term target.* Tao's logarithmically-averaged two-point Chowla
  (arXiv:1509.05422) proves `Σ_{n≤x} λ(n)λ(n+2)/n = o(log x)`; the **log-weight `1/n` is
  essential** (the entropy-decrement argument needs it), and the **Cesàro (natural
  density) two-point Chowla is still open**, let alone the uniform-in-`d` version C5
  demands. The specific named obstruction to de-averaging is the **random-graph /
  expansion step** that the entropy-decrement method leaves open (Tao states the averaging
  "cannot currently be removed"). This is concrete and honest, but it is a famous frontier
  problem; we have **no new handle** on the expansion step that the literature lacks.
  Attacking it head-on is legitimate but would be attacking a known wall with no new tool —
  which the campaign verdict suggests is the kind of move that returns "still C5".

  *Net on (b):* both break conditions are **correctly localized and honestly stated**, but
  both are **at the hardness of C5 itself** under current tools; I do not have, and the
  campaign did not produce, a new instrument to apply to either. I would not represent (b)
  as tractable.

#### (c) The independence direction (exit 3)

*Assessment: this is NOT a known approach with a handle; it is itself a famous open
meta-problem with no current method. I will not oversell it.*

- A true `Π₂`-arithmetical sentence being **unprovable in PA** (or a fragment) would
  require a method to certify unprovability of a statement whose truth is itself unknown.
  The closest thing in the literature (Hamkins et al., arXiv:2110.08640, "Is the twin
  prime conjecture independent of Peano Arithmetic?") shows one can construct an
  arithmetical formula that **ZF proves is independent of PA and that is (extensionally)
  equivalent to TPC** — but **only** because the truth value of that formula cannot
  currently be established in ZF or any trusted metatheory. That is a logician's
  *cautionary* construction, **not** an independence proof of TPC: it does not tell us TPC
  is independent of PA; it tells us we cannot presently rule it out, which is a statement
  about our ignorance.
- More bluntly: there is **no known technique** for proving a specific, believed-true
  `Π₂` number-theoretic statement like TPC unprovable in PA, let alone in ZFC. Genuine
  PA-independence results (Paris–Harrington, Goodstein, Kirby–Paris hydra) attach to
  statements *engineered* to encode fast-growing / transfinite combinatorics with no such
  number-theoretic anchor; TPC has the wrong shape for those methods, and no one knows how
  to bridge the gap. Pursuing (c) would mean **trying to invent the missing meta-method
  from scratch** — itself a celebrated open problem in mathematical logic. Under
  AGENTS.md, (c) is a legitimate *exit-direction* only if it reaches a **rigorous**
  independence proof; until then it is OPEN, and it has **no current handle**. I record it
  honestly as such and decline to manufacture optimism about it.
